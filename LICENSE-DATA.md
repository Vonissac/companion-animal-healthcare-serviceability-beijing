# Data Use Terms

This repository contains derived research data created for scholarly verification of the associated manuscript.

The derived data may be used for non-commercial research, teaching and review purposes with appropriate citation of the associated article and repository. Redistribution of the derived data should preserve this notice and the source-boundary notes in `docs/DATA_SOURCES_AND_BOUNDARIES.md`.

The repository does not grant rights to redistribute or reconstruct restricted third-party raw datasets. Users who need raw source data should obtain them from the original providers under their own terms.
